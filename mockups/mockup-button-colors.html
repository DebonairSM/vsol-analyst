Design Changes

  • Send Button: Changed to green (#16a34a) - the only distinctive color
  • Polish Button: White background with dark blue border (#1e40af) - clean and simple
  • All Other Buttons: Use shades of blue from the salt blue palette
  • Color Hierarchy: Darkest blue (#1e40af) for Extract, medium blues for uploads, lighter blue (#60a5fa) for Voice
  • Consistent Shadows: All buttons use matching shadow colors based on their background
  • Visual Cohesion: Buttons now form a harmonious blue gradient with Send standing out in green
